Frequently Asked

How is a "sample" counted?

One sample equals one upload — either a microscopy image (morphology) or a video of up to 60 seconds (motility). Re-analyses of the same file within 24 hours are free.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Lab subscriptions are month-to-month with no cancellation fee. Enterprise contracts follow the signed SLA term.

Do you offer academic or veterinary-school discounts?

Yes — non-commercial research labs receive a 40% discount on Lab plans. Contact us with institutional verification.

Where is my data stored?

EU data centers by default, with end-to-end encryption and auto-purge after 180 days unless archive storage is enabled. On-prem deployment is available on Enterprise.
